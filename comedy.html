Comedy

Because actuaries can be funny too... occasionally... with a standard deviation of humor.

About This Section

Coming soon: a collection of original stand-up clips, actuarial humor, and jokes that only math nerds would understand. This page will eventually include recordings of my amateur stand-up attempts and written jokes that find the humor in the seemingly dry world of actuarial science.

Why Comedy?

Humor is a powerful way to make complex ideas more accessible and memorable. As someone who spends a lot of time with mathematical concepts, finding ways to communicate these ideas through humor is both challenging and rewarding.

Plus, actuaries have a reputation for being... well, not the life of the party. Consider this my small contribution to dispelling that stereotype.

Actuary Walks Into a Bar...

Here's a sample of the kind of humor you can expect in the future:

An actuary, an underwriter, and a claims adjuster walk into a bar. The bartender says, "Is this some kind of joke?" The actuary replies, "I calculate a 95% probability that it is."

Stay tuned for more intentionally nerdy humor and maybe even some recorded performances once I work up the courage to do an open mic night.
