About This Site

Welcome to my digital garden of self-directed learning in actuarial science. This site serves as a collection of resources, thoughts, and materials that I've found helpful in my journey of becoming an actuary.

Unlike a traditional blog with chronological posts, this is organized more like a garden — a space where ideas grow over time. Feel free to explore the different sections, from book recommendations to exam preparation resources.

This site is constantly evolving as I continue my learning journey. Check back regularly for new content and updates!
