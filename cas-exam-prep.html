CAS Exam Preparation

This section contains my study notes, resources, and strategies for Casualty Actuarial Society (CAS) exams. I'm documenting my preparation process to solidify my understanding and help others on the same path.

Exam Structure

The CAS credential requires passing a series of exams covering topics in probability, financial mathematics, statistics, insurance ratemaking, reserving, and more. The general progression is:

  • Preliminary Exams (P, FM, IFM, STAM, LTAM, SRM)
  • CAS-specific Exams (MAS-I, MAS-II)
  • Associate Level Exams (5-7)
  • Fellowship Level Exams (8-9)

Study Resources

My go-to resources for exam preparation include:

  • Coaching Actuaries - Adapt practice exams and Learn modules
  • The Infinite Actuary - Video lessons and practice problems
  • ACTEX/ASM Study Manuals - Comprehensive textbooks with practice problems
  • CAS Exam Guide - Official syllabus and past released exams
  • Marcel Finan's Notes - Free online materials for preliminary exams
