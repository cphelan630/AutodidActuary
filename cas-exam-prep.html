CAS Exam Preparation

Resources, strategies, and notes for Casualty Actuarial Society exams

Actuarial exams can be tough to get started on because all the prep material is so expensive, leading to a selection bias in the field. To avoid this and contribute to the community, I'll develop and share some study materials that I come up with. You shouldn't need to pay so much for a subscription when you're pre-entry level.

Exam Structure

The CAS credential requires passing a series of exams covering various topics in property and casualty insurance, including:

  • Probability & Financial Mathematics
  • Mathematical Statistics
  • Regression and Generalized Linear Models
  • Time Series
  • Statistical Learning
  • Basic Ratemaking
  • Estimating Claim Liabilities
  • And more specialized topics in upper-level exams
