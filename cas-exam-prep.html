CAS Exam Preparation

Resources, strategies, and notes for Casualty Actuarial Society exams

This section contains my study notes, resources, and strategies for Casualty Actuarial Society (CAS) exams. I'm documenting my preparation process to solidify my understanding and help others on the same path.

Exam Structure

The CAS credential requires passing a series of exams covering various topics in property and casualty insurance, including:

  • Probability & Financial Mathematics
  • Financial Economics
  • Models for Financial Economics
  • Construction and Evaluation of Actuarial Models
  • Basic Ratemaking
  • Estimating Claim Liabilities
  • And more specialized topics in upper-level exams

Here are some resources I've found helpful in my exam preparation:
