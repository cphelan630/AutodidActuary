Book Reviews

Discover books that have shaped my understanding of actuarial science

My Reading List

Loss Models: From Data to Decisions

By Stuart A. Klugman, Harry H. Panjer, Gordon E. Willmot

A comprehensive resource that balances mathematical rigor with practical applications. This book covers everything from frequency and severity modeling to credibility theory.

Actuarial Mathematics for Life Contingent Risks

By David C. M. Dickson, Mary R. Hardy, Howard R. Waters

An excellent introduction to life contingencies modeling. The text is clear and provides intuitive explanations of complex concepts.

Derivatives Markets

By Robert L. McDonald

The definitive guide to understanding financial derivatives. McDonald presents complex topics with excellent examples and clear explanations.
